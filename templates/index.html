Intro

Hi, I'm Brian.

I am a biomedical scientist with a strong background in experimental design and data analysis of high throughput disease models using Python and R. I'm passionate about applying programming & data science to address biological questions.

Close

Work

Since early 2022, I've been developing next-generation human disease models at MIMETAS, first as a junior and since 2024, as a Scientist. I have contributed to the development of highly automated, patient-derived, cancer and gut-on-a-chip models.

In addition to my research activities as a Scientist, I have collaborated with our data scientists to develop Python software pipelines that enhanced data processing and analysis, significantly improving the efficiency of our workflows.

Before embarking on my masters degree, I joined the Hospital Epidemiology department at the University Hospital Basel as an assistant clinical and scientific researcher. During this time I co-first authored a study on surgical site infection risk across health insurance classes.

Close

Education

In late 2021, I graduated with honors from the Master’s program in Life Science and Technology at TU Delft. I specialized in quantitative modeling of metabolic networks, synthetic biology, and programming. During a 6-month industry placement at Roche in Basel, I developed a complex personalized in vitro model to capture & predict adverse cancer therapy events.

I hold a BSc in Biomedical Sciences from the University of Fribourg, Switzerland (2018), where I gained foundational knowledge in medicine, research, and quantitative methods.
